# Sales-Performance-Trend-Analysis-Using-Python-Pandas


## Project Overview

The **Sales Performance & Trend Analysis Using Python (Pandas)** project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to uncover meaningful business insights related to revenue trends, product performance, customer demand, and time-based sales patterns.

The project was implemented using **Python and Pandas** to clean, transform, and analyze raw sales data, enabling structured decision-making through data-driven insights. The analysis helps stakeholders understand sales behavior over time and identify high-performing products and categories.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue distribution
* Identify top-performing products based on sales and quantity
* Understand monthly and yearly sales trends
* Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to assess data quality
* Support business decision-making through aggregated insights

---

## Data Preparation & Exploration

* Imported and explored the sales dataset using **Pandas**
* Reviewed:

  * First and last records of the dataset
  * Dataset shape and column structure
  * Data types and missing values
  * Descriptive statistics (mean, min, max, etc.)
* Verified **order date ranges** to understand the sales timeline
* Extracted **month and year** from order dates for time-based analysis

**Outcome:** Ensured data quality and readiness for accurate analysis.

---

## Key Analysis & Insights

### 1️⃣ Time-Based Sales Analysis

* Extracted **month-year** from order dates
* Aggregated sales by month to identify revenue trends
* Identified periods of high and low sales performance

**Business Value:** Helps management plan seasonal promotions and forecast demand.

---

### 2️⃣ Product-Level Performance Analysis

* Grouped data by **product name** to calculate total sales
* Identified top-performing products contributing maximum revenue
* Analyzed products based on **quantity sold**

**Business Value:** Supports inventory planning and bestseller identification.

---

### 3️⃣ Category & Sub-Category Analysis

* Grouped sales by **category and sub-category**
* Compared revenue contribution across different product segments

**Business Value:** Helps prioritize high-margin categories and optimize product mix.

---

### 4️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Conducted summary statistics and aggregation analysis
* Visualized sales distributions and grouped metrics
* Identified patterns, trends, and outliers in sales data

**Business Value:** Converts raw data into actionable insights for stakeholders.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

* **Matplotlib / Seaborn** – Data visualization
* **Jupyter Notebook** – Analysis and reporting
